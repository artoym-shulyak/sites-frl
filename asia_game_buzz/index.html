ABOUT US

Asia Game Buzz is a PR company specializing in marketing Steam games to Asian audiences - China, Korea, Japan, and Southeast Asia.

Asia Game Buzz helps video game developers and publishers reach a broader and more engaged audience in the booming Asian video game market.

As a specialized games marketing agency, we craft tailored strategies to boost visibility, engage audiences, and drive success for game developers. From targeted campaigns and influencer partnerships to innovative digital strategies, we power up your game's presence in the competitive gaming landscape.

Chinese market is a key market for us. China serves as the focal point of our strategic initiatives, embodying a key market for our marketing agency due to its immense growth potential, expansive consumer base, and strategic significance in the global business landscape.
